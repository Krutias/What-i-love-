About BTS

BTS (Bangtan Sonyeondan), also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band formed in 2013 under BigHit Entertainment (now HYBE). The group consists of seven members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook.They became globally famous for their powerful music, meaningful lyrics, and strong connection with fans. Their songs often talk about self-love, mental health, youth struggles, and personal growth.
